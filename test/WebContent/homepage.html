Bangladesh Army University of Engineering & Technology (BAUET) has been established by the Bangladesh Army with due permission from the government of the Peoples Republic of Bangladesh with a view to imparting Quality Education at affordable cost to students of rural areas in the higher Education Arena. As it has been totally patronized and controlled by the Bangladesh Army, this institution will very soon be built up its image like other Army Educational Institutions. If the guardians get their wards admitted, I can assure them that, they will not be deceived. Rather, they will pass out with Standard Educational degrees which will definitely help them to get established in life and enlighten the society with enriched knowledge and wisdom. We welcome Co-operation from all in this regards.
